COMPREHENSIVE ANALYTICS FEATURES FOR GARAGE ECOMMERCE PLATFORM

1.  OVERALL BUSINESS PERFORMANCE DASHBOARD

-   Total revenue (daily, weekly, monthly, yearly)
-   Number of orders (products vs services)
-   Gross profit and net profit
-   Platform commission earned
-   Active users (buyers, vendors, service providers)
-   Conversion rate (visitors to paying customers)
-   Average order value (AOV)
-   Growth rate trends (month-over-month, year-over-year)

2.  SALES & REVENUE ANALYTICS

-   Revenue by category (spare parts, equipment, services)
-   Revenue by vendor/service provider
-   Top-selling products and services
-   Seasonal demand trends
-   Payment method performance
-   Revenue by location

3.  PRODUCT & INVENTORY ANALYTICS

-   Stock levels and stock-out frequency
-   Fast-moving vs slow-moving items
-   Dead stock tracking
-   Product return rates and reasons
-   Pricing performance analysis
-   Supplier/vendor performance

4.  SERVICE PERFORMANCE ANALYTICS

-   Number of bookings per service type
-   Most requested services
-   Service completion rate
-   Average service time
-   Cancellation rates and reasons
-   Service provider ratings and reviews
-   Revenue per service provider

5.  CUSTOMER ANALYTICS

-   New vs returning customers
-   Customer lifetime value (CLV)
-   Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
-   User behavior funnel (browse to purchase)
-   Customer journey tracking
-   Demographics (location, device)
-   Retention and churn rate

6.  VENDOR & SERVICE PROVIDER ANALYTICS

-   Top-performing vendors/service providers
-   Revenue contribution per vendor
-   Order fulfillment rate
-   Delivery/service delays
-   Ratings and feedback trends
-   Vendor churn
-   Compliance metrics

7.  ORDER & TRANSACTION ANALYTICS

-   Total number of orders
-   Order success vs failure rate
-   Cart abandonment rate
-   Average order processing time
-   Refund and dispute rates
-   Delivery timelines

8.  MARKETING & TRAFFIC ANALYTICS

-   Traffic sources (organic, social, ads)
-   Campaign performance
-   Cost per acquisition (CPA)
-   Click-through rates (CTR)
-   Search terms used on platform
-   Most viewed products/services

9.  FINANCIAL & COMMISSION ANALYTICS

-   Platform earnings (commissions, fees)
-   Vendor/service provider payouts
-   Outstanding balances
-   Profit margins per category
-   Tax calculations

10. OPERATIONAL ANALYTICS

-   System performance (load times, downtime)
-   Support tickets and resolution times
-   Common customer complaints
-   Fraud detection indicators
-   Failed transactions

11. PREDICTIVE & AI ANALYTICS

-   Demand forecasting
-   Price optimization suggestions
-   Inventory restocking recommendations
-   Customer purchase predictions
-   Vendor performance forecasting
-   Personalized recommendation engine

12. CUSTOM REPORTS & FILTERS

-   Date range filters
-   Filter by vendor, category, service type
-   Export reports (PDF, Excel)
-   Scheduled reports

13. VISUALIZATION & UI FEATURES

-   Interactive charts
-   Heatmaps
-   Drill-down capability
-   Real-time updates
-   Comparison views

14. ALERTS & NOTIFICATIONS

-   Low stock alerts
-   Sales drop alerts
-   High cancellation alerts
-   Vendor underperformance alerts
-   Fraud/suspicious activity alerts
