# MYGARAGE HOMEPAGE REDESIGN SPECIFICATION (/)

Redesign the MyGarage homepage into a premium automotive marketplace experience focused on helping customers quickly discover, compare, and purchase vehicle spare parts, accessories, and automotive services.

The homepage should feel like a modern, high-trust automotive marketplace where users immediately begin searching, browsing, and exploring products.

Primary objectives:

* Encourage purchases
* Improve product discovery
* Make finding car parts fast and simple
* Build trust through clear product, supplier, and compatibility information
* Maximize product visibility without clutter or empty layouts

Design direction:

* Premium
* Compact
* Professional
* Marketplace-first
* Conversion-focused

The homepage uses a single scroll experience with a centered content area (max width ~1500px).

---

1. HOMEPAGE STRUCTURE

The homepage contains four main sections:

1. Featured automotive products
2. Sponsored deals / promotions
3. Smart category product discovery feed
4. Infinite product browsing feed

The experience must prioritize products and shopping actions over marketing content.

Avoid:

* Large hero banners
* Excessive explanatory text
* Generic landing page content
* Empty whitespace caused by low product counts

Focus on:

* Products
* Prices
* Vehicle compatibility
* Suppliers
* Fast discovery

---

# SECTION A: FEATURED AUTOMOTIVE PRODUCTS

Purpose:
Immediately surface high-demand and high-value automotive products.

Layout:

Desktop:

A premium split layout:

Main spotlight product:

* Featured badge
* Product name
* Vehicle compatibility / model fit

Showcase sponsored supplier listings and promotional products.

Compact horizontal carousel.

Each slide includes:

* Product image or banner

* Vehicle compatibility
* Category
* Price

* View Product

---

# SMART CATEGORY GROUPING & LAYOUT OPTIMIZATION (IMPORTANT)

The layout must intelligently combine categories based on available products.

Rules:

* Do not force every category into its own full-width row
* Categories with fewer than 3 products should share horizontal space with other small categories
* Multiple small categories can appear side-by-side on desktop
* Larger categories may span full width when needed
* Maintain clear category labels above each group

Example:

Instead of:

Brake System
(2 products → wasted space)

Electrical Parts
(2 products → wasted space)

Display:

Brake System        Electrical Parts
[Product][Product]   [Product][Product]

Goal:

* Eliminate empty-looking sections
* Maximize visible products per screen
* Maintain category clarity without wasting space

Desktop:

* Use flexible grid/masonry-like layout

Mobile:

* Stack categories vertically for readability

# SECTION D: INFINITE PRODUCT DISCOVERY FEED

Purpose:
Provide continuous browsing of automotive products.

Initial load:

* 3 categories
* 5 products per category

Dynamic loading:

* Load additional categories/products when user reaches page bottom
* Use Intersection Observer or equivalent lazy loading

States:

Loading:

* Minimal spinner or skeleton loader

Error:

* Retry option

Empty:

* "No products available"

End of feed:

* "You have reached the end"
